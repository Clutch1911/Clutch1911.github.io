Michael Robert Merola

I'm a Colorado native hailing from snowy Boulder, CO. I am a student, musician, developer, fencer, artist, actor, and thinker. This webpage presents the details of myself, my work, and my interests. Contact me if you want to talk! Thanks.

Currently, I am a second-year Computer Science major at the University of Colorado Boulder. I can program in C++, Python, Javascript, and HTML/CSS. I have experience in the workforce from previous internships with Hewlett-Packard Enterprise and Schendt Engineering. I work at CU's Office of Data Analytics on the Virtual Reality Research team. We are working to develop a collaborative virtual space for students. I like to use Unity 5 and GameMaker Studio to learn about game development, and I am learning how to develop web apps using React.

I have attended four hackathons. My first was the Broncos TackleSTEM hackathon in Denver, where my team and I won the 'Best Demo' Award. In March, my team and I attended the CalTech hackathon and won 'Best Use of AWS' for our Seedling project. Recently, we flew to Philidelphia to participate in the nationally-recognized UPenn hackathon. Check out my Devpost and GitHub for more info on my projects.

One of my greatest passions is music. I have been an avid guitarist since age 10, playing everything from Led Zeppelin to Wes Montgomery. I also like to play clarinet, bass guitar, piano, and some saxophone. I currently play in the CU Boulder Jazz ensembles and you can occassionally catch me playing at jam sessions around Boulder! Additionally, I sing in CU's own 'One Note Stand' A Capella group. When I have some free time, I love to record covers of my favorite songs and work on my first self-produced original album.

To keep being physically active, I like to fence epee in the CU Fencing Club!

Acting is another passion of mine. I love to act in plays and sometimes musicals. In high school, I performed as a supporting role in Neil Simon's The Odd Couple and as the lead role of Pip in Charles Dickens' Great Expectations. Although I am focusing more on my music in college, I would love to participate in Boulder's Shakespeare Festival one day!
